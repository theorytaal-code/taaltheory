Performance Setup Requirement

For delivering the best possible sound quality and live performance experience, we kindly request that the basic sound infrastructure be arranged by the event organizer.

This includes:
• Professional Sound System
• Quality Microphones
• Speakers & Monitors
• Mixer Console
• Stage Setup & Power Supply

Having proper equipment ensures powerful audio clarity, better audience engagement, and a high-energy performance experience.
